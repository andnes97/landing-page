CRaP Climbing

Despite our name, we are proud to be the biggest climbing community in this hypothetical city. We do weekly trips with our members both to the gym and outdoors. We go bouldering, rope climbing, or we'll just hang out in nature.

If this sounds like something for you, please feel free to sign up and join our thrill seeking community today.

Benefits for club members

Members get a 10% discount in the local outdoor shop.

Members have priority access to any upcoming training and courses.

We have weekly trips to the different nearby climbing gyms.

Every month we have a road trip, finding different outdoor climbing spots and challenges.
